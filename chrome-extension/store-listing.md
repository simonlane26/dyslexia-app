## Long description
Dyslexia Write is a distraction-free writing space designed for people who find writing difficult — especially those with dyslexia.

Open the extension whenever you need to draft an email, write a message, or compose anything in your browser. Your work saves automatically and stays there next time you open it.

**Features:**

- Dyslexia-friendly fonts — Arial, Verdana, Comic Sans, Georgia
- Adjustable font size (12–28px)
- Background colour choices — cream, white, light blue, mint, yellow, dark
- ✨ AI Simplify — paste complicated text and get a plain-English version instantly
- 🔊 Read aloud — hear your writing read back to you at a comfortable pace
- Auto-saves your draft as you type
- Word count
- Works on any website — emails, forms, social media, documents

**Who is it for?**
People with dyslexia, reading difficulties, or anyone who wants a calmer, more accessible writing experience in their browser.

**Privacy**
Your draft is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage — it never leaves your browser. The AI Simplify feature sends only the text you choose to simplify to our server (dyslexiawrite.com) — your text is never stored or used for training.
